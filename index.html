We Are Here To Help Power Millions Of People in 100+ Countries

A mobile application that monitors the spread of the COVID-19 virus and assesses potential cases for spread prevention.
The application maintains added features for self-evaluation, news updates from government-approved sources, purchases for provisions, medical care alerts, and self-care information, etc.

How it works

Here Are Some Of The Major Features

Integrated Symptom Self-checker

This allows a user to self-check their symptoms via a checklist and also select which symptoms appeared and in which order and the app alert the individual to either self-quarantine at home or report to the nearest healthcare facility and sends alert to healthcare authorities with family information as well.

Self-care Medical Kits

Users can order self-care medical kits that include sanitizers, antiseptics, gloves, medication, vitamins, etc from selected government health care stores and pay via Virtual Cash / Mobile money, visa, etc.

Live Virus-based News Updates

The app presents virus-based news updates using Coronavirus ad words identification via Artificial Intelligence to search for information from primary news outlets such as UN, WHo, government, etc and news.

Government Facility Updates

The app can receive direct source alerts from the government on major details such as curfews, numbered cases, and new directives and measures.

Emergency Center

The app can make a call for an ambulance or emergency services at the user’s selection.

Measure Alerts

The app maintains a list of preventive measure alerts for different situations as sourced from WHO or UN
